Afya Flavors Cafe

Welcome to Afya Flavors Cafe, where we serve authentic Kenya's delicious Meals and Drinks in a friendly, welcoming environment. Located in the heart of Kerugoya, we are here to satisfy your cravings! Whether you're in the mood for a hearty breakfast, a flavorful traditional lunch, or a refreshing drink, our menu is carefully crafted to bring you the best of Kenyan cuisine. Every dish is made with fresh, high-quality ingredients to ensure a delightful experience with every bite. Come and enjoy a warm atmosphere, excellent service, and the true taste of Kenya.
